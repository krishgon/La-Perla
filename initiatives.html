Initiatives

Covid 19 safety measures
Please keep a distance of 1.5 meters from other guests and hotel employees
Our public spaces are regularly disinfected and aerated. Liquid disinfectant is at your disposal
Your room is cleaned daily and the surfaces are disinfected.
Social distancing is enforced between the restaurant tables.
Only 16 people are currently allowed in the swimming pool and 10 people in the spa.
The reception offers you hygiene kits (disinfectant, mask, etc.)
Social Work

Supporting local charities and organisations with their environmental and ethical campaigns.

Ensuring everyone within our teams understands and contributes to our initiatives.

Conscientious stewardship of our estates including monitoring flora and fauna, planting native species, developing wildlife habitats and safeguarding areas of special interest.

Minimising our environmental impact, by reducing waste, improving energy efficiency, and conserving and recycling materials.

Nurturing the communities around us by sourcing their quality local produce, and helping protect their natural environment, prosperity and amenities.

Encouraging our suppliers to adopt our positive environmental practices.
