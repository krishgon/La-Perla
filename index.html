THE STORY OF TAJ

01
The Idea

At Taj, we understand that our story is simply one of many that emerge out of a crisis.The idea is to create a peaceful and tranquil setting; a dreamy escapade through a one-of-a-kind journey.

We believe the ultimate luxury is connection. The resort and the experience have been designed to allow our guests to reconnect with nature, with their loved ones and most importantly, with themselves.

The Hotels

Meticulously designed by internationally acclaimed architects, Taj hotels are designed to be in complete harmony with nature. Glorious views come as standard in all of our five-star hotels and luxury villas.

The Culinary

Foodie heaven awaits you at Taj, where five-star restaurants sit side by side with traditional taverns, serving the freshest and tastiest cuisine.
